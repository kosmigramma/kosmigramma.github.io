Cardtables Online

Cardtables Online is a way to play any cardgame with your friends using a virtual cardtable.

This is how it works:
  1. You create a room with a shareable link.
  2. Share the link with your friends and play your favorite cardgame using a virtual deck!
  3. You can even communicate using webcam/microphone with members of the table.
