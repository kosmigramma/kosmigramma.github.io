Play a Cards Against Humanity clone online with your friends with webcam and microphone support!

This is how it works:
  1. You create a room with a shareable link.
  2. Share the link with your friends to play together.
  3. You can communicate using webcam/microphone with members of the room and the members faces appear in their seats, making the experience feel just as if you are playing in real life!
